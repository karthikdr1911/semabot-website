Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

semaBot ("we", "our", or "us") operates an AI-powered WhatsApp mentor for entrepreneurs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use semaBot, we collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages you send to semaBot during your session
  • Basic interaction metadata (timestamps, message status)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Deliver AI-powered coaching and mentoring responses via WhatsApp
  • Maintain conversational context within your active session
  • Improve the quality and reliability of the service

3. Data Storage

We do not persistently store your messages or conversation history in our database. Messages are processed in real time to generate responses and are not retained after your session ends.

4. Third-Party Services

To operate semaBot, we share data with the following third parties:

  • Meta (WhatsApp) — Your messages are transmitted through the WhatsApp Business Platform, governed by Meta's own privacy policy.
  • Anthropic / OpenAI — Message content is sent to AI model providers for processing and response generation. These providers may retain data per their own policies.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption and access controls to protect data in transit. We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of the service

7. Children's Privacy

semaBot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of semaBot after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at karthik@semabot.in.
